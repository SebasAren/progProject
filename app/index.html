Internet connectivity around the world

Information

In this visualization you can see the ways in which the quality of internet connections can be a good estimate for the overall economic situation in a country.

Besides economic factors you can also take a look at factors used by the Happy Planet Index and potential correlations between internet quality and these indexes.

Data
  • Various internet quality parameters
  • Nominal GDP:
Usage

You can drag and drop countries from the datamap to the individual plots. You can change the information displayed by pressing the buttons. You can also move countries between plots by pressing the arrow buttons. Deleting items from the plots is done by clicking the respective datapoint or bar.
